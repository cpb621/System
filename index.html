Progression

Double progression. Each lift has a rep range. Add reps until every set hits the top, then add weight and restart at the bottom. Beat the logbook somewhere every session. Log every set in Hevy.

Blocks are 5 weeks. Weeks 1–4 climb. Week 5 deload: same lifts, 2 easy sets each. Then Block 2: core lifts stay, accessories rotate.

Week 1 is calibration. Stop every set 2–3 reps shy of failure while finding working weights. Real climbing starts week 2.

Banned for life: barbell back squat, barbell bench, pull-ups, lunges, split squats. Built for 6'9" leverages on purpose.

Cooking
Salsa verde chicken pressure cooker
  1. 3 lbs chicken breast in the pot
  2. Full 12 oz jar TJ green salsa + big pinch salt
  3. High pressure 12 min, natural release 5, then vent
  4. Shred with two forks, stir into the juices
  5. Portion. The sauce keeps it juicy all week
Taco beef griddle
  1. 3 lbs 80/20 ground beef, brown and break up
  2. Drain only some fat
  3. Taco seasoning + splash water, 2 min
  4. Portion
White rice pressure cooker
  1. 4 cups rice, rinsed
  2. 1:1 water, pinch of salt
  3. High pressure 4 min, natural release 10
  4. Reheat rule: splash of water, lid cracked
Breakfast burritos batch of 10–12
  1. Griddle diced onions + peppers
  2. 1.5 lbs breakfast sausage
  3. Scramble 18 eggs
  4. Assemble: tortilla, eggs, sausage, veg, cheese
  5. Roll tight, wrap in foil
  6. 2–3 fridge, rest freezer (~1 month)
  7. Reheat: unwrap, damp paper towel, 90–120 sec
Ranch chicken pasta dinner A
  1. Boil 1 lb pasta
  2. In hot pot: pasta + shredded chicken + ranch + splash whole milk
  3. Big handfuls spinach, wilts and disappears
  4. Shredded cheese, portion
Taco beef pasta dinner B
  1. Boil 1 lb pasta
  2. Mix with taco beef + green salsa + cheese
  3. Portion
Griddle potatoes
  1. Dice, toss in olive oil + salt
  2. Griddle till crispy, or 425°F oven ~30–35 min
Cook days
Sunday big cook ~90 min · feeds Mon–Wed
  1. Pressure cooker: salsa verde chicken first, it runs itself
  2. Griddle zone 1: taco beef
  3. Griddle zone 2 / oven: potatoes + veg pan at 425
  4. Pressure cooker round 2: rice
  5. Griddle last: eggs + sausage, roll the burrito batch, foil, freeze most
  6. Portion containers + mini bowls, fridge, done
Wednesday restock ~40 min · feeds Thu–Sun
  1. Fresh rice batch
  2. One protein round
  3. Rebuild mini bowls, burrito top-up from freezer
  4. Fresh produce if needed
Shelf life law: cooked chicken, beef, rice, pasta = 3–4 days in the fridge. Two cook days exist so nothing you eat is ever older than 4 days. Frozen burritos = 1 month.

The two anchors

1. Wake at 8:00 every day. Weekends too. Alarm across the room.

2. The phone never crosses the mattress. Dock it, then bed. Every night.

Standing rules

Checklist order is law. Top to bottom, no skipping. Nothing else exists until the last box is checked.

Phone is a tool until the morning list is done. Alarm, music, MacroFactor. No socials, no texting back.

Water: a gallon a day = 5 Hydro fills. Two of them at the gym.

Log everything: every meal in MacroFactor, every set in Hevy, weigh-in every morning.

Revise after living in it, not before. Adjustments happen at block changes and real schedule changes.

Minimum viable morning

If a day blows up: weigh-in · make bed · water · dog walk · shake · gym. Do those and the day still counts. Then get back on the full list tomorrow.
