Progression

Double progression. Each lift has a rep range. Add reps until every set hits the top, then add weight and restart at the bottom. Beat the logbook somewhere every session. Log every set in Hevy.

Blocks are 5 weeks. Weeks 1–4 climb. Week 5 deload: same lifts, 2 easy sets each. Then Block 2: core lifts stay, accessories rotate.

Week 1 is calibration. Stop every set 2–3 reps shy of failure while finding working weights. Real climbing starts week 2.

Recipe cards · exact portions
Breakfast burritos ×12 700–850 kcal
  1. Per burrito: 2 eggs · 2 oz cooked sausage · 100g hash browns · 28g cheese · 1 large tortilla
  2. Sausage first, weigh it cooked
  3. Scramble 24 eggs slightly soft, they finish in the reheat
  4. Hash browns cooked until browned so nothing goes soggy
  5. Tortilla on the scale, tare, build to the exact amounts
  6. Roll tight, wrap in foil, label with date
  7. Don't overfill, overstuffed burritos burst in the microwave
  8. 2–3 to the fridge, freeze the rest
Beef rice bowls ×6 850–1,050 kcal
  1. Per bowl: 227g cooked 93/7 beef · 300g cooked rice · 100g broccoli · 28g cheese
  2. Brown ~4 lbs beef, taco seasoning + garlic + onion powder
  3. Drain only if really greasy, some fat is calories
  4. Container on the scale, tare, build each one identical
  5. Green salsa stays out until after reheating
Chicken bowls ×6 850–1,000 kcal
  1. Per bowl: 227g cooked chicken · 300g cooked rice · 100g corn · 28g cheese
  2. Season ~5 lbs breast: olive oil, salt, pepper, garlic, onion, paprika
  3. Bake at 425°F until 165°F internal, thermometer in the thickest piece
  4. Rest 10 min, dice, portion on the scale
Protein pasta ×3 800–1,000 kcal
  1. Per bowl: 300g cooked pasta · 5–6 oz beef · 125–150g sauce · big handful spinach · 28g cheese
  2. Boil 1 lb pasta, brown the pasta's share of the beef
  3. Sauce into the beef, spinach in until it wilts and disappears
  4. Pasta in, splash of pasta water if thick, portion into 3
  5. Pasta lives in the fridge, freezing wrecks the texture
Rice batch pressure cooker
  1. Jasmine rice for 12 bowls plus buffer, rinsed, 1:1 water, pinch of salt
  2. High pressure 4 min, natural release 10
  3. Weigh 300g cooked into every bowl

The two anchors

1. The day starts the moment you wake: no phone in bed, feet on the floor, checklist from step 1.

2. The phone never crosses the mattress. Dock it, then bed. Every night.

Standing rules

Checklist order is law. Top to bottom, no skipping. Nothing else exists until the last box is checked.

Phone is a tool until gym + reset is done. Music and MacroFactor only. No socials, no texting back.

Water: a gallon a day = 5 Hydro fills. Two of them at the gym.

Log everything: every meal in MacroFactor, every set in Hevy, weigh-in every morning.

Revise after living in it, not before. Adjustments happen at block changes and real schedule changes.

Minimum viable morning

If a day blows up: weigh-in · make bed · water · dog walk · burrito · gym. Do those and the day still counts. Then get back on the full list tomorrow.
